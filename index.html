Our Mission

We believe that artificial intelligence (AI) will fundamentally change our world. In such a world, it is crucial that the next generation is empowered to harness AI for good. Our mission is to do just that by providing free, high-quality AI workshops and resources for students of all skill levels and interests, engaging guest speaker panels with AI experts, and mentorship for student-led AI projects aimed at benefiting their communities.
